Contrast

ETQ Amsterdam

etq-amsterdam.com

This website followed the principle of contrast. Against the dark background are light-colored images and font, which can easily be recognozed byt the viewer.

Hick's Law

Superlist Website

superlist.com

Hick's law makes the choice easier for users. This page presents few buttons and links, so it is easier for the users to browse it.

Fitts Law

Church of Jesus Christ Website

churchofjesuschrist.org

Fitts Law deals with the time spent in moving from one distance to another. This website takes only a shorter time to move from one point to another. The buttons are all on the top and the page contains few pictures and links. It only took two fast scrolls in the mobile phone to get to the end of the page.
